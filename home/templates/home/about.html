About Us

Background, Qualifications and Experience

With 20 years experience teaching Physical Education in Ireland, I constantly came across students and athletes who were ill-informed about the proper nutrition needs and requirements for their own individual situation. So often, I heard stories of misguided coaches, parents giving advice that was not evidence-based. I completed a Masters. I have worked with Galway WFC, Galway Corinthians Rugby, Connacht Women's Rugby, Colga FC, Bray Emmets GAA, individual athletes.

We offer detailed guidance on the following topics:
  • Best foods for muscle recovery
  • High protein diet for athletes
  • Foods that boost endurance
  • Carbohydrates for athletes
  • Sports nutrition for teenage athletes
  • Recovery protocol for athletes
  • What to eat before a workout
